Join

If you're interested in joining CUAC, we'd love to meet you! Come along to any one of our taster sessions in the first few weeks of term, and be sure to meet us at Fresher's fair (we'll be the one in the infamous Cambridge Blue kit with the wreaths on waving our banners proudly in the air!), or at one of our social events we'll be hosting especially for newcomers.

Specifically, if you would like to keep up to date with the latest goings-on with CUAC but do not want to join immediately, please register your details with Daisy at dei23@cam.ac.uk and we will keep you updated with our training schedule by email. As it may take a few days to register your details please check our club calendar regularly for upcoming training session, events and competitions.
